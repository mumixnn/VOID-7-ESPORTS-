About Us

VOID 7 ESPORTS (V7) is a professional esports organisation based in Srinagar, Kashmir. The team currently competes in Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) and focuses on disciplined team play, player development, and competitive excellence. V7 aims to represent Kashmir on the national esports stage while contributing to the growth of esports culture in the region.

Founded in Kashmir

Established in Srinagar to highlight and develop esports talent from the region.

Competitive BGMI Roster

Actively competing in BGMI with a focus on performance and professionalism.

Building Esports Culture

Committed to growing and supporting the esports ecosystem in Kashmir.
